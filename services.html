Get to know region better

Very crucial element when establishing or simply travelling is getting familiar with history and culture of the region. Pirkanmaa is a region rich in history, culture, interesting places and beautiful landscapes.

Tampere is the second biggest city in Finland and is potentially the first location that you might want to visit. However, this is not the only place worth visiting to deepen knowledge and connect with nature.

Have you ever heard about the Mannerheim Statue in Leinola district in Tampere? Do you know why this monument stand on the hill in the forest?

Or have ever visited Pirunlinna ancient castle on the hill that was said to be built by the devil?

You can see those and many other interesting spots with help of Team Bridgease who are in good terms with history nerds, who are willing to share their knowledge and experiences with anyone.
