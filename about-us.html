About Us

Bridgease is a travel and event solution provider focusing on business clients. Our mission is to provide quality immersive experiences that combine cultural and professional interests.

Bridgease is a website for the companies, start-ups and other firms interested in discovering more about the city of Tampere for business.

Our goal is to help our customers to plan their visits effectively by providing information, booking hotels or venues, providing personalized content that convinces clients on why they should be coming to learn more about the unique culture and industries that Tampere offers.

Bridgease was created from the initiative of eight International Business students from Tampere University of Applied Sciences in September 2024.

We combine experiences and knowledge of our team members with extensive research, to make sure that what we give others, is what we want to receive.
